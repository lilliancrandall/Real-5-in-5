5 in 5

Each day, I receive a notification with a headline news story from the New York Times. For my 5 in 5 project I took a screenshot of the first alert I received each day and created an illustration through a Mandala technique using the Apple Pencil and iPad Pro.

  • Constraint: Using the radial symmetry style of the Mandala Technique create an illustration
  • Conditional: The New York Times headliner
